Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

🔒 Short version: Geo Alert doesn't collect anything about you. All your data stays on your phone. No servers, no tracking, no surprises.

What Geo Alert does

Geo Alert is a location-based alert app. You mark a place on the map, configure the radius, and the app notifies you when you're nearby. That simple.

What we DON'T collect

Let's be straightforward:

  • 🚫 Your location — it's processed on your phone and that's it. It doesn't go anywhere.
  • 🚫 Personal data — we don't ask for name, email, phone number, nothing.
  • 🚫 Analytics — no tracking, no pixels, nothing monitoring you.
  • 🚫 Usage data — we don't know what you do in the app. And we don't want to.

What stays on your phone

The app saves some data locally on your device:

  • 📍 Your saved places (name, position, radius)
  • 🔔 History of alerts that have fired
  • ⚙️ Your preferences (language, theme)

This data never leaves your phone. If you uninstall the app, it's automatically deleted.

External services

The app uses two Google services for one thing only: searching addresses.

  • Google Places / Geocoding — when you search for an address to add a place, the text you typed is sent to Google to find the location. That's it, only at that moment.
  • Apple Maps / Google Maps — to display the map. It only receives the visible region on screen.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions

The app only asks for what's necessary:

  • 📍 Location — to know when you're near a saved place, even with the app closed.
  • 🔔 Notifications — to notify you when an alert fires.
  • 📳 Vibration — for vibration-type alerts.

You can revoke any permission in your phone settings at any time.

Security

Your data is protected by your phone's operating system (iOS or Android). We recommend keeping a password or biometrics enabled on your device.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

Changes to this page

If we change anything important here, we'll let you know in an app update.
